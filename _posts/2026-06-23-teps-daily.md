The World Wildlife Fund (WWF) has identified several species of big cats as being threatened by the loss of their natural habitats. The primary threat to these majestic predators is the destruction of their habitats, which is largely due to human activities such as deforestation and urbanization. As a result, several species of big cats are on the brink of extinction. The WWF has urged governments and individuals to take immediate action to protect the natural habitats of these species. In particular, the organization has called for the establishment of protected areas and the implementation of sustainable land-use practices. By taking these measures, we can help to conserve these magnificent creatures and ensure their survival for future generations.

(B) 사람들의 관심을 돌리기 위해

**문제 1 정답:** (D) 인간 활동의 영향을 줄이기 위해 — 인간이 활동하는 곳에서 사자가 살기 어려움에 따라 사자를 보호하기 위해 인간 활동의 영향을 줄여야 한다.
