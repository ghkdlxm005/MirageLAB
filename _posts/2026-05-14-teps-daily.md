## 오늘의 TEPS 단어 (5개)

**1. diversity** (di-va-rsi-tei) _명사_
- 뜻: 다양성
- 예문: The company celebrates its diversity by hosting a multicultural festival every year.
- 해석: 회사에서는 매년 다문화 페스티벌을 개최해 다양성을 기념한다.

**2. doubt** (daut) _명사_
- 뜻: 의심
- 예문: Her ability to solve the puzzle was doubted by everyone.
- 해석: 그녀의 퍼즐을 풀 수 있는 능력에 대해 모두 의심했다.

**3. sovereign** (sov-ri-n) _명사_
- 뜻: 군주
- 예문: The sovereign of the kingdom visited the capital city.
- 해석: 왕은 왕국을 방문했다.

**4. resources** (ri-sors) _명사_
- 뜻: 자원
- 예문: The company has limited resources to invest in new projects.
- 해석: 회사에 새로운 프로젝트에 투자하기에 충분한 자원이 없다.

**5. gradually** (gre-di-ool-lei) _부사_
- 뜻: 서서히
- 예문: The weather will gradually get colder as winter approaches.
- 해석: 겨울이 다가오면서 날씨는 서서히 추워지게 된다.

## 오늘의 독해

**지문:**

The concept of sovereignty has been debated among scholars and political scientists for centuries. Some argue that it is the absolute power of a state to govern its territory without external interference, while others see it as a more nuanced concept that encompasses various forms of authority and control. Despite these differences, many agree that sovereignty is essential for a state's ability to protect its citizens and provide essential resources. However, the rise of globalization has led to an increase in international cooperation and interdependence, which can sometimes undermine a state's sovereignty. As a result, some countries are gradually redefining their concept of sovereignty to accommodate these changing circumstances.

**문제 1.** Sovereignty is closely related to which of the following concepts?

(A) Totalitarianism
(B) Globalization
(C) International cooperation
(D) Democracy

**문제 2.** What is happening to some countries' concept of sovereignty due to globalization?

(A) It is becoming more absolute
(B) It is becoming more limited
(C) It is becoming more nuanced
(D) It is becoming obsolete

**올바른 예문:**
